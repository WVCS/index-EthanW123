C O A C H E L L A

Coachella has become the bread and butter of music festivals. Known for its stacked, star-studded lineups, fashion trends and hippie-style camping experience in the deserts of Indio, the festival has developed a culture of its own.
Ticket prices vary. General admission passes are $429 plus fees and the VIP festival passes are $999 plus fees.
